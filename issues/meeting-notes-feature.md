### Title: Add feature to capture meeting notes and generate actionable items

**Description:**
As a user, I want Jarvis to have the ability to listen during a meeting, identify the participants, take intelligent notes, and prepare a list of actionable items for post-meeting follow-up. This feature should:
- Automatically detect participant names (if some are unknown, leave them blank and add an actionable to complete the list of participants).
- Record meeting notes accurately.
- Analyze the meeting for key action points and generate a clear, accessible list of actionable items.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] Ability to listen to audio input and process speech into text.
- [ ] Detect and attempt to identify participants in the meeting.
- [ ] Create a structured list of notes, highlighting action items.
- [ ] Append an actionable item for completing any unidentified participants.
